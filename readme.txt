Easily manage your videos from Vippy.co on your WordPress site.

== Description ==

The Vippy plugin for WordPress allows you to easily manage your videos from Vippy.co on your WordPress site.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the Vippy plugin to your site.

2. Activate it. 

3. Enter your Vippy.co credentials on the Vippy settings page.
